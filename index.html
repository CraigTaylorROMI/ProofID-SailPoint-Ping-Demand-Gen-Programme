Summary of topics (value optimisation)

These are the core optimisation themes we’ll use to educate, diagnose, and convert installed-base accounts.

  • Expensive software (big investment) – expected ROI often isn’t realised
  • Lacking in-house skills
  • Complexity of deployments
  • Hidden costs of SailPoint/Ping deployment
  • Never-ending implementations
  • Connecting/onboarding enterprise apps for true ROI – the long tail of applications
  • Understanding how to integrate the apps you’re onboarding
  • Platform under-utilisation – missing new features (e.g., AI/automation)
  • DevOps: managing in configuration (not code) can make it harder to adapt

Key principle: account-level engagement matters. We want more than one person per account. The more personas engaging and the more signal categories triggered, the higher the intent.

Signal sources (account-level intent)

  • HubSpot Visitor Intent (first-party): reverse-IP + intent rules (e.g. pricing / key pages repeat visits)
  • HubSpot Research Intent (third-party): topic + competitor research signals across the web
  • External “News” signals: funding, leadership changes, growth/expansion, business risk indicators
  • Assessment behaviour: assessment start + completion (email required to start)
  • LinkedIn engagement: connection accepts, replies, comments, profile views, clicks to assessment page
